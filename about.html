Rachael Lam

I am a current Master's of Information student at the University of Toronto with a concentration in Human-Centred Data Science. I completed my undergradute degree in California and majored in Africana Studies. I'm deeply interested in the socio-cultural implications of data and how to better represent marginalized communities. My biggest educational accomplishement so far is being the first person to receive 100% on a paper that has been completed by 300+ students.

Additionally, I have brought lean startup methodology to the art industry, which has traditionally suffered from high overhead costs.

I'm originally from Vancouver. I love making kombucha and reading books that border prose. If you're looking for a new book, I highly recommend The God of Small Things by Arundhati Roy.
