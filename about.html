Rachael Lam

My journey to data science and product has not been straightforward. I found my niche as an Operations and Product Manager at a travel startup, favouring the product cycle that allowed me to bring all my siloed knowledge together to build impactful products. I then co-built a product that supported language learners through immersion methodologies. Through these experiences, I saw first hand how data could improve a startup and advance my own projects and was eager to learn more.

I’m now pursuing my Masters of Information in Human-Centred Data Science, bridging all my seemingly disjointed interests and experiences together, from my undergrad in Africana Studies to my experience as a product manager and co-founder. I’m ensuring I constantly reflect of the socio-cultural implications of data and how to better represent marginalized communities. I know this understanding and passion is necessary and beneficial for the future of data and product development.
